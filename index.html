Introduction

The Rhythm Thief Fan Sequel is a passion project that is being made by fans for fans! Raphael's adventure, which ended in quite the cliffhanger, made players anxious for a sequel that never arrived. We are trying to continue Raphael's story.

This all started with a comment on a YouTube video made by the current project manager, Kopi Mopi.

Narrative

Raphael can finally retire from being the thief we all know, instead focusing his life around investigating his father. Nevertheless, a month later, Raphael reads a newspaper article about an art theft that wasn't his. The police seem to think it was though.

The Phantom R needs to clear his name and get to the bottom of this!
